Top Influencers

Chiara Ferragani

Chiara Ferragni is an Italian entrepreneur, fashion blogger, influener, and designer. Her blog called The Blonde Salad has allowed her to collaborate with many fashion and beauty brands. In 2017, Forbes ranked her the "Top Fashion Influencer. Chiara has an extremely impressive career starting in 2009 with the launch of her blog. By 2011, she was recognized by New York Magazine as one of the biggest break out street-style stars of the year. From there her followers began to multiply and she gained worldwide recognition as a fashion icon. Within just two years of the launch of her blog it gained 1 million unqiue visitors and 12 million views per month. Throughout her career she has one countless blogger related awards that validate her global popularity and strong voice in the fashion industry. Another noteworthy accomplishment is in 2013 she published an ebook version (in italian only) of her blog The Blonde Salad. In terms of a modeling carrer, she was the star model for Guess in November of 2013 for their ad campaign. She was also chosen to be on the cover of Vogue Espana in 2015 and became the first blogger to be featured on the cover of any Vogue magazine. Since he Vogue cover in 2015, Ferragni has been featured on over 50 magazine covers. Chiara has also been extremely successful with her personal business ventures. Her line Chiara Ferragni Collection, started as a footwear line and has expanded into various fashion departments from denim to beachwear. Her business is worth over 30 million euros! As one of the most influential people in fashion, her social media presence is extremely important. With over 17 million followers on instagram specifically, she uses her platform effectively and efficiently to communicate her strong message to the masses. In interviews Chiara explains her posting strategy as instinctive. She has said, "If I feel I want to do something, I do it." This philosphy has made her account raw and real and allowed her followers to connect on a more personal and deeper level.

“I would like to create something that I can wear and my mother can wear and my younger sisters can wear,” Ferragni says of her interest in the family brand. Are we talking about a desire to emulate the Kardashians here? She laughs. “Maybe — in the future”.
