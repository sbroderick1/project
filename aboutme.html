About Me

Style/Aesthetic:

I am a designer whose main goal is to make women feel confident, sexy and beautiful. My aesthetic is classic and simple but with some edge or unique design element. I gravtiate towards luxurious fabrics and colors over vibrant patterns as I prefer the style of the garment to be the statement not the fabric. In terms of designing, I have been focusing on bringing lucury to loungewear for my first fashion line. I think this would be the perfect line to launch my brand.

Experience in the Fashion Industry

I began to take my passion for fashion design seriously in high school. My junior and senior year I worked on an indpendent study where I developed a 12 piece collectiom that was displayed in our schools art showcase. I wa able to showcase my full development process including inspiration, sketches, fabric selection, product construction, fittings, alterations, and photoshoot. Since then I have continued to sketch and contruct garments in any free time that I have as a full time student-athlete. I have interned at a local boutique in Wellesley, MA called Anna Nieman Couture as a marketing and design intern where I was able to assist the designer with sourcing fabric, client consultations, increasing exposure, gainig market share, and updating the website and social media plaforms. I have also showcased my designs in a school fashion show. I have been offered a merchandising position at TJX for after graduation which I have excepted and am excited for role in maintaining the sucess of the company.

Aspirations

As a rising senior at Babson College, I am getting my Bachelors Degree in General Business Administration with a concentration in Retail Supply Chain Management. After graudation I was offered an entry level job as an allocation analyst at TJX Companies. My hope is to begin my career at TJX, a well established and large company, to gain lots of real world exposure, gain my footing in the industry and learn all the inner workings of how to run a successful fashion business. As you will see below I have been designing clothes since high school. Since my return from my semester abroad in Milan, I have been working on the development and launch of my fashion line. This fashion line will be a true representation of my aesthetic and vision as a designer. This is a very large project and of course will take time. I plan to work on this for the first few years afer graduation as a side job. I will be working at TJX during the day and in my own time working to grow my fashion line into a successful and strongly desired company. My hope is that one day I can have my own company and be a designer/entrepreneur/business woman/style icon.
