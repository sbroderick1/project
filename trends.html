Summer 2020 Trends

Linen

Linen fabrics can keep you cool in the summer heat! This season retailers have been looking to expand on the traditional linen pants and incorporate the faric in all garment types.

Sweat Shorts

Loungwear and sweatpant sets have become increasingly popular given the state of quarantine that the country has been in. Now entering summer, it is too hot to wear heavy sweatpants and sweatshirts. The sweat shorts are a great transition for summer maintaing that comfy, cozy athleisure style whithout leaving you sweating.

Baby Doll Dresses

Mini babydoll dresses have been another popular trend this summer! Their loose fitting quality keeps you light and breezy. These dresses are playful, comfy and stylish and can be accessorized for any occasion whether its going to the grocery store or going on a dinner date. Expect to see these dresses in pastel colors and with carying volumes throughout the summer months.

Neon

The past yera we have also seen the rise of neon. And this trend has only become increasingly more popular this summer. The style lesson with neon is dont be afraid to stand out and make a statement!

Bralettes

Bralettes under blazers is possibly the biggest trend to hit the runway for spring/summer 2020. Street style influencers have quickly stolen this trend from the runway and made it an everyday, ready-to-wear ensemble that is casual but sexy. Retailers are offering a surplus of bralette designs to give customers lots of options. Its so versitle and can be used in just about any look!

Puff Sleeves

Puff sleeves was a 2019 trend that is still living on into the summer of 2020! This silhouette is 80s inspired and can elevate any simple, plain shirt from basic to unique and fashion forward.
